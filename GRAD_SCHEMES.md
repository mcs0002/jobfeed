# Graduate Schemes — Apply Directly (Not on ATS Boards)

Firms that run structured graduate or internship programmes with a **single programme page, unified deadline, and direct application form** — not as rolling individual job postings on an ATS board. The scraper's job board will miss these or give a misleading picture. Apply directly through the programme URL.

Firms marked 🎓 on the Sources page are already in the scraper's `targets.json` and appear here with their dedicated programme page. Firms in the "Not in scraper" section have no ATS entry yet and must be tracked manually.

Convention: `"grad_scheme": true` and `"grad_scheme_url"` in `targets.json` wires up the 🎓 marker on Sources.
